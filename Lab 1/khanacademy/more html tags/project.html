Recipe for salmon fried in a frying pan with broccoli

  • Prep:1 min
  • Cook:5 mins
  • Serves 2
Ingredients Quantity
salmon fillets 2
olive oil ½ tbsp
unsalted butter 20g
lemon, juiced ½

Step 1: Generously season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Put the oil and butter in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat, swirling around the pan until melted and foaming, then turn up the heat.

Step 3: Once the butter starts bubbling, add the salmon fillets to the pan, skin-side-down, and fry for 3 mins until crisp.

Step 4: Flip the fillets over, lower the heat and cook for 2 mins more, then drizzle with the lemon juice.

Step 5: Transfer the salmon to a plate and baste with any of the buttery juices left in the pan.

Recipe of Chicken satay salad with pomegranate seeds

  • Prep:15 mins
  • Cook:5 mins - 10 mins
  • Serves 2
Ingredients Quantity
tamari 1 tbsp
medium curry powder 1 tsp
ground cumin ¼ tsp
garlic clove, finely grated 1
clear honey 1 tsp
skinless chicken breast fillets 2
crunchy peanut butter 1 tbsp
sweet chilli sauce 1 tbsp
lime juice sunflower oil, for wiping the pan 1 tbsp
Little Gem lettuce hearts, cut into wedges 2
cucumber, halved and sliced ¼
banana shallot, halved and thinly sliced coriander, chopped 1
pomegranate seeds ½

Step 1: Pour the tamari into a large dish and stir in the curry powder, cumin, garlic and honey. Mix well.

Step 2: Slice the chicken breasts in half horizontally to make 4 fillets in total, then add to the marinade and mix well to coat.

Step 3: Set aside in the fridge for at least 1 hr, or overnight, to allow the flavours to penetrate the chicken.

Step 4: Meanwhile, mix the peanut butter with the chilli sauce, lime juice, and 1 tbsp water to make a spoonable sauce.

Step 5: When ready to cook the chicken, wipe a large non-stick frying pan with a little oil.

Step 6: Add the chicken and cook, covered with a lid, for 5-6 mins on a medium heat, turning the fillets over for the last min, until cooked but still moist. Set aside, covered, to rest for a few mins.

Step 7: While the chicken rests, toss the lettuce wedges with the cucumber, shallot, coriander and pomegranate, and pile onto plates. Spoon over a little sauce.

Step 8: Slice the chicken, pile on top of the salad and spoon over the remaining sauce. Eat while the chicken is still warm.

Recipe of Tomato & pasta soup

  • Prep:5 mins
  • Cook:25 mins
  • Serves 4
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 2 tbsp
onion, chopped 1
celery sticks, chopped 2
garlic cloves, crushed 2
tomato purée 1 tbsp
chopped tomatoes 400g
chickpeas 400g
orzo or other small pasta shapes 150g
vegetable stock 700ml
basil pesto 2 tbsp
crusty bread, to serve to choose from

Step 1: Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large saucepan. Add the onion and celery and fry for 10-15 mins, or until starting to soften, then add the garlic and cook for 1 min more.

Step 2: Stir in all the other ingredients, except for the pesto and remaining oil, and bring to the boil.

Step 3: Reduce the heat and leave to simmer for 6-8 mins, or until the pasta is tender. Season to taste, then ladle into bowls.

Step 4: Stir the remaining oil with the pesto, then drizzle over the soup. Serve with chunks of crusty bread.
